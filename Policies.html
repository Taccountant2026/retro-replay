Policies

Warranty

All RetroReplay consoles include a limited 30‑day functional warranty. These devices are 20–25 years old; minor age-related quirks may occur. Warranty covers major functional failures only (disc drive, power issues). Cosmetics and laser aging are not covered.

Returns

Returns accepted within 14 days if the item is not as described. Buyer pays return shipping unless fault is confirmed.

Shipping

All consoles are shipped in impact-protected packaging with tracking.

Refunds

Refunds processed within 3–5 business days.
