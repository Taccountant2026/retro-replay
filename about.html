Meet the Founder

Meet the Founder

Leighton Hepworth

Hello! I’m Leighton Hepworth, founder of Retro Replay. Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science & Technology, I’ve been passionate about consoles and tech from childhood.

Retro Replay was born from my love for gaming hardware, restoration, and creative mods. Every console refurbished and every bundle curated reflects this dedication.

Our Mission & Vision

We aim to bring retro consoles back to life, giving both nostalgia lovers and new gamers a high-quality experience. Every product is tested, cleaned, and upgraded where possible, ensuring it exceeds expectations.

Our vision is to become the premier destination for retro gaming, offering not just consoles, but experiences—memories, craftsmanship, and reliability.
