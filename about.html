About Retro Replay

Discover the passion, story, and mission behind restoring classic consoles.

Meet the Founder

Hello! I’m Leighton Hepworth, the founder of Retro Replay. Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science & Technology, my passion for consoles and technology has been with me since childhood.

Retro Replay is my way of combining my love for gaming, hardware restoration, and modding, providing retro enthusiasts with refurbished consoles, curated games, and professional upgrades.

My Journey

From playing classic consoles in my youth to studying technology at university, I’ve dedicated myself to understanding gaming hardware inside and out. Every console restored and every mod crafted reflects this passion.

Through Retro Replay, I aim to deliver not just consoles, but experiences—nostalgia, craftsmanship, and quality for gamers worldwide.
