Cookie Policy

This policy explains what cookies are, which cookies we use on Retro Replay, and how you can control them.

Quick summary: We only use non-essential cookies (analytics) if you accept them via the banner.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember information about your visit (for example, preferences) and can be used for analytics.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies / local storage

We use basic storage needed for site features you use (for example, remembering your cookie preference and cart/session functionality if enabled). These are required for the website to function properly.

Analytics cookies (optional)

If you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics to measure traffic and improve the website (for example, which pages are visited most and how visitors move through the site).

Category Purpose Optional?
Essential Remember your cookie choice and enable basic site functions (and cart/session features if used). No
Analytics Understand site usage and improve performance and content (Google Analytics). Yes

Your choices

  • You can accept or reject non-essential cookies using the cookie banner.
  • If you previously chose, you can change your choice by clearing site data (local storage) in your browser and reloading the website.
