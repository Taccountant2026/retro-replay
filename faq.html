Frequently Asked Questions

Do your consoles work with modern TVs?

Yes! Our PS2s are fully compatible with SCART, component cables, or HDMI adapters. You can enjoy crisp visuals whether on classic CRTs or modern HD displays.

Do they play PS1 games?

All PS2 models support PS1 discs flawlessly, allowing you to relive classic PlayStation titles with perfect compatibility.

What condition are your consoles in?

Every console is fully refurbished and tested. Exteriors are cleaned and polished, lasers are calibrated, and minor age marks are noted honestly. Each unit is ready to play or display for collectors.

Can I mod or upgrade my console?

Yes! We offer a variety of upgrades including FreeMcBoot, HDD setup, OPL, and even cosmetic restoration. Modded consoles are fully tested for safe and reliable use.

Do you offer warranty?

All refurbished consoles come with a standard Retro Replay warranty covering functionality. Modded units have warranty terms on software and hardware upgrades.

How do I buy or trade in my PS2?

Check our Trade-In page to send us your console for a fair quote, or visit our Buying Guide to choose the right model for you.

Are your products authentic?

All consoles are original PlayStation 2 hardware, sourced responsibly. Retro Replay is not affiliated with Sony, but we take pride in preserving authentic PS2 gaming experiences.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We carefully package consoles and accessories to ensure they arrive safely anywhere in the world.
