Our FAQs

What is a refurbished console?

A refurbished console has been professionally cleaned, tested, and restored to working order. All our consoles come with a 6-month warranty.

How long does delivery take?

UK orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. International orders may take 7-14 days depending on your location.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary by location. Check your cart for exact rates.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day returns policy for peace of mind. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund or exchange your console.

Do you offer mods and upgrades?

Yes! We offer HDD installations, softmods, FMCB setup, and other customizations. Select these options when ordering or contact us for details.
