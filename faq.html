Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know Before You Buy

What warranty do your refurbished consoles come with?

Every console includes a 6-month hardware warranty covering faults, failures, and defective parts. Optional extended warranties are available on the Warranties page.

How do your refurbishments work?

Each console undergoes a full 15-step refurbishment process: deep cleaning, thermal renewal, laser rebalancing, PSU testing, pad rebuilds, cosmetic refresh, full diagnostics, and more.

Visit our full refurb process for detailed steps.

Can I trade in my old console?

Yes! You can trade in PS1/PS2/PS3 consoles and accessories. Visit the Trade-In page to submit details and receive a valuation.

Do you offer modifications or upgrades?

Yes — from HDD upgrades to fan mods, RGB improvements, custom shells, softmods, and full restoration packages.

See the Mods & Upgrades page for all available options.

How long does shipping take?

UK delivery is typically 1–3 business days via tracked services. Worldwide shipping is available on request.

Do you accept returns?

Yes — returns are accepted within 14 days if the device is in the same condition received. Warranty returns are handled separately under our repair guarantee.
