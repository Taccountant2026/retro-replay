How We Refurbish

Complete, documented process for every console. Professional restoration to like-new condition.

Our 7-Step Refurbishment Process

  • 1. Complete Disassembly - Carefully take apart console, noting each component for proper reassembly
  • 2. Ultrasonic Cleaning - Deep clean all internal components using ultrasonic technology to remove decades of dust and grime
  • 3. Laser/Optical Drive Calibration - Test and calibrate disc readers; replace laser if needed to ensure perfect disc reading
  • 4. Thermal Refresh - Replace thermal paste, tune fans, and ensure optimal cooling for longevity
  • 5. Power Supply Inspection - Check capacitors, test voltage output, and replace power supply if needed
  • 6. Play Testing - Full system test with original games to ensure everything works perfectly
  • 7. Secure Packaging - Carefully reassemble, package securely, and ship with tracking and insurance

Quality Standards

Grade A - Like New

Minimal cosmetic wear, looks nearly new. Best for collectors seeking pristine condition consoles.

Grade B - Excellent

Minor cosmetic wear but fully functional. Great balance of affordability and condition.

Grade C - Good

Visible cosmetic wear but fully functional. Perfect for gamers who prioritize gameplay.
