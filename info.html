Warranty & Returns

All consoles come with a **30-day warranty** covering faults, disc read errors, HDD issues and internal failures. Accidental damage or misuse is not covered.

We accept returns within 30 days. Buyer covers return postage unless the item arrives faulty.

Shipping Information

All orders are shipped from the UK via Royal Mail or Evri depending on package size.

  • Consoles: 2–4 day delivery
  • Accessories: 1–3 days
  • Tracked shipping included on all orders

Order Tracking

You will receive a tracking number via email once your console has been dispatched.

For custom orders, you will receive progress updates throughout the build.
