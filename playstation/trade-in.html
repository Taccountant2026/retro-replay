Trade In Your PS2

Retro Replay loves giving old PlayStation 2 consoles a new life. Whether your system is fully functional, slightly worn, or completely broken, we can offer refurbishment or fair trade-in value.

We Accept

  • PS2 Fat Consoles – all models including early and late variants
  • PS2 Slim Consoles – SCPH-7000x and later
  • Controllers, memory cards, and official accessories
  • Faulty consoles – dead laser, no power, broken shell, overheating, etc.

How It Works

Send us the model number, condition, included accessories, and ideally photos. We'll assess and offer a fair quote depending on refurbishment potential.
