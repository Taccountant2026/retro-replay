2. Definitions

  • Refurbishment: deeper internal servicing/restoration beyond standard preparation, only where explicitly purchased.
  • Upgrade/modification: optional changes such as storage upgrades, configuration, video accessories, or cosmetic enhancements.
  • Built-to-order / Custom: items assembled, configured, upgraded, or sourced specifically to your request.
  • Arrival guarantee: confirmation your item arrives working as described (see Warranty page for full coverage).

3. Products & Condition

Retro Replay specialises in professionally tested, cleaned, and prepared retro gaming consoles and accessories. Unless explicitly stated as new, items are pre-owned.

  • Each console is function-tested prior to dispatch (power, output, ports, basic operation).

  • Minor wear consistent with age should be expected on pre-owned items.
  • Images may be representative; exact cosmetic marks can vary between units.
  • If you require specific cosmetic requirements, request a sourcing/custom build before purchase.

4. Preparation, Refurbishment & Upgrade Services

Some products include standard preparation by default. Optional refurbishment and upgrades may be available and will be described at the point of purchase or confirmed in writing.
