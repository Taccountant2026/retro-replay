Terms & Policies

Important information about purchases, services, shipping, returns, and warranties.

Last updated: January 2025

For warranty details, see Warranty. For delivery times & shipping info, see Delivery.

1. Introduction

These Terms & Policies govern purchases, services, and use of the Retro Replay website. By ordering, requesting sourcing, or using this site, you agree to these terms.

2. Products & Condition

Retro Replay specialises in professionally tested, cleaned, and prepared retro gaming consoles and accessories. Unless explicitly stated, all items are pre-owned.

  • Each console is function-tested prior to dispatch.
  • Cosmetic condition is graded transparently (A / B / C) where applicable.
  • Minor wear consistent with age should be expected on pre-owned items.
  • Images may be representative; exact cosmetic marks can vary between units.

3. Refurbishment & Upgrade Services

Optional refurbishment, upgrades, and modification services may be available and are described at the point of purchase.

  • Premium refurbishment involves deeper internal servicing, cleaning, and restoration processes.
  • Upgrades may include storage upgrades, software setup, video accessories, or cosmetic enhancements.
  • Once a refurbishment/upgrade service has been completed, it may be non-reversible.
  • Service pricing reflects labour, parts, testing time, and preparation.

4. Software, Modifications & Homebrew

Some products/services may include configuration for homebrew or user-supplied backups. Retro Replay does not provide, supply, or endorse copyrighted game files.

  • Customers are responsible for ensuring legal use in their jurisdiction.
  • No copyrighted content is included unless explicitly stated.
  • Support is limited to supplied hardware/functionality, not third-party software behaviour outside our control.

5. Sourcing Requests

Our sourcing service helps locate specific console revisions, cosmetic grades, or bundles.

  • Sourcing is subject to market availability.
  • Quoted prices may change if market conditions change before confirmation.
  • Where applicable, sourcing fees cover time and procurement effort.
  • Custom-sourced items may have limited return eligibility where permitted by law (e.g., where customised to your request).

6. Pricing & Payment

All prices are shown in GBP unless stated otherwise.

  • Prices include applicable preparation and testing costs.
  • Shipping costs (if any) are shown separately.
  • Payment must be received in full before dispatch.

7. Shipping & Delivery

We ship using tracked and insured delivery services wherever possible.

  • Dispatch and delivery times are estimates, not guarantees.
  • Customers must provide accurate delivery information.
  • Risk transfers upon confirmed delivery, subject to applicable consumer protections.

8. Warranty & Guarantees

All consoles include an arrival guarantee confirming the item arrives in working condition. Extended warranty options may be available separately.

  • Warranty covers functional hardware faults only.
  • Warranty does not cover accidental damage, misuse, liquid damage, or unauthorised modifications/repairs.
  • Cosmetic wear is not covered.

9. Returns & Cancellations

Returns are handled in line with UK consumer regulations where applicable.

  • Customised or upgraded items may be exempt from standard returns where permitted by law.
  • Returned items must be in the same condition as supplied, with included accessories.
  • Shipping costs may be non-refundable unless the item is faulty or misdescribed.

Retro Replay shall not be liable for indirect or consequential losses (e.g., loss of data, loss of enjoyment, or third-party software issues) except where liability cannot be excluded by law.

For questions regarding these terms, contact us via the Contact page.
