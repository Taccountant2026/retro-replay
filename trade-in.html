Trade In Your PS2

Retro Replay loves giving old PlayStation 2 consoles a new life. Whether your system is fully functional, slightly worn, or completely broken, we can offer refurbishment or fair trade-in value.

We Accept

  • PS2 Fat Consoles – including early and mid-generation models
  • PS2 Slim Consoles – SCPH-7000x series
  • Controllers, memory cards, and accessories
  • Damaged or faulty consoles – broken lasers, scratched panels, or non-working units

How It Works

Send us details about your console using the form below. Include model, condition, any included accessories, and photos if possible. We’ll respond with a fair quote, reflecting the potential for refurbishment or resale.
