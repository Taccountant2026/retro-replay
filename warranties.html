Warranties & Coverage

All Retro Replay devices are pre-tested and refurbished to work reliably. Due to the age of these consoles and accessories, our warranty covers only the minimum to ensure fair replacement in the rare case of faults.

What’s Covered

  • Replacement of defective consoles or accessories within 14 days of delivery.
  • Malfunctions discovered on arrival, not caused by misuse, drops, or external damage.
  • Limited support for functionality verification or troubleshooting advice.

What’s Not Covered

  • No refunds under any circumstances.
  • Damage caused after delivery (drops, spills, electrical surges, etc.).
  • Cosmetic wear and tear that does not affect functionality.
  • Software or game-related issues.

How to Claim

If you receive a device with a defect covered under warranty, please contact us immediately with your order details, photos, and a description of the issue. We’ll arrange a replacement where applicable.
